## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit `http://localhost:3000`
2. Click **Sign Up** in the top right
3. Fill in your details:
   - Username
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 6 characters)
4. Click **Create Account**
5. You'll receive a welcome email
6. You're ready to shop!

### Logging In

1. Click **Login** in the top right
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Sign In**
4. You'll be redirected to the home page

### Guest Shopping

You can browse products without an account, but you'll need to create an account to:
- Add items to cart
- Make purchases
- Track orders
- Save wishlist items
- Write reviews

## Shopping Cart

### Adding Items to Cart

1. Browse to a product
2. Select size/color (if applicable)
3. Click **Add to Cart**
4. Item is added to your cart
5. Continue shopping or proceed to checkout

### Viewing Your Cart

1. Click the cart icon in the top right
2. You'll see all items in your cart with:
   - Product image
   - Name and details
   - Price
   - Quantity
   - Subtotal

### Updating Cart

**To change quantity**:
1. Use the +/- buttons next to each item
2. Cart total updates automatically

**To remove an item**:
1. Click the **Remove** button
2. Item is removed from cart

### Cart Summary

Your cart shows:
- Subtotal: Total before shipping
- Shipping: Delivery cost
- Total: Final amount to pay

## Checkout Process

### Step 1: Review Cart

1. Click **Proceed to Checkout**
2. Review all items in your cart
3. Verify quantities and prices
4. Make any final changes

### Step 2: Shipping Information

Enter your delivery address:
- Full name
- Street address
- City
- State/Province
- Postal code
- Phone number

**Tips**:
- Double-check address accuracy
- Include apartment/unit number
- Provide working phone number
- Save address for future orders

### Step 3: Payment Method

Choose your payment method:
- **Cash on Delivery**: Pay when order arrives
- **Bank Transfer**: Transfer to provided account
- **Credit/Debit Card**: (If available)

### Step 4: Review Order

Before placing your order, review:
- Shipping address
- Payment method
- Order items
- Total amount

### Step 5: Place Order

1. Review terms and conditions
2. Click **Place Order**
3. You'll receive order confirmation
4. Check your email for order details

## Order Tracking

### Viewing Your Orders

1. Click on your profile icon
2. Select **My Orders**
3. You'll see all your orders with:
   - Order number
   - Order date
   - Total amount
   - Current status
   - Tracking information

### Order Statuses

Your order progresses through these stages:

1. **Pending**: Order received, awaiting seller confirmation
2. **Confirmed**: Seller confirmed your order
3. **Processing**: Seller is preparing your order
4. **Shipped**: Order has been shipped
5. **Delivered**: Order delivered to you

### Tracking Your Order

1. Go to **My Orders**
2. Click on an order
3. View detailed status
4. See estimated delivery date
5. Track delivery progress

### Order Details

For each order, you can see:
- Order number
- Order date
- Items ordered
- Quantities and prices
- Shipping address
- Payment method
- Current status
- Tracking number (when shipped)

## Wishlist

### What is a Wishlist?

A wishlist lets you save products you're interested in for later. Perfect for:
- Saving items for future purchase
- Creating gift lists
- Comparing products
- Tracking price changes

### Adding to Wishlist

1. Browse to a product
2. Click the **♡ Add to Wishlist** button
3. Item is saved to your wishlist
4. Heart icon turns solid ♥

### Viewing Your Wishlist

1. Click **Wishlist** in the navigation
2. See all saved items
3. View product details
4. Add items to cart
5. Remove items you're no longer interested in

### Managing Wishlist

**To add to cart from wishlist**:
1. Go to your wishlist
2. Click **Add to Cart** on any item
3. Item moves to your cart

**To remove from wishlist**:
1. Go to your wishlist
2. Click **Remove** on any item
3. Item is removed from wishlist

## Product Reviews

### Why Write Reviews?

Reviews help:
- Other customers make decisions
- Sellers improve products
- Build community trust
- Share your experience

### Writing a Review

**To review a product**:
1. Go to **My Orders**
2. Find a delivered order
3. Click **Write Review**
4. Rate the product (1-5 stars)
5. Write your review
6. Click **Submit Review**

### Review Guidelines

**Good reviews include**:
- ✓ Honest opinion
- ✓ Product quality details
- ✓ Fit and sizing info
- ✓ Pros and cons
- ✓ Photos (optional)

**Avoid**:
- ✗ Offensive language
- ✗ Personal attacks
- ✗ Spam or ads
- ✗ Fake reviews

### Reading Reviews

Before purchasing:
1. Check product reviews
2. Read recent reviews
3. Look at star ratings
4. Check verified purchases
5. See what others say

## FAQ

### Shopping Questions

**Q: Do I need an account to shop?**  
A: You can browse without an account, but need one to purchase.

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**  
A: Yes, we use secure payment processing and encryption.

**Q: Can I cancel an order?**  
A: Yes, if it hasn't been shipped yet. Contact support immediately.

**Q: How long does shipping take?**  
A: Usually 3-7 business days, depending on location.

**Q: Do you ship internationally?**  
A: Currently, we only ship domestically.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**  
A: Cash on Delivery, Bank Transfer, and Credit/Debit Cards.

### Account Questions

**Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?**  
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow instructions.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**  
A: Yes, update it in your profile settings.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**  
A: Contact customer support to request account deletion.

**Q: Why can't I login?**  
A: Check your email and password. Try resetting your password.

### Order Questions

**Q: Where is my order?**  
A: Check "My Orders" for tracking information.

**Q: Can I change my shipping address?**  
A: Only before the order ships. Contact support immediately.

**Q: What if my order is damaged?**  
A: Contact support within 48 hours with photos.

**Q: Can I return a product?**  
A: Yes, within 30 days if unused and in original packaging.

### Product Questions

**Q: Are product images accurate?**  
A: We strive for accuracy, but colors may vary slightly.

**Q: How do I know my size?**  
A: Check the size guide on each product page.

**Q: Are products authentic?**  
A: Yes, all products are verified by our team.

**Q: Can I request a product?**  
A: Contact support with your request.
