## Getting Started

### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Go to `http://localhost:3000/login`
2. Click **Sign Up**
3. Fill in your details:
   - Username
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 6 characters)
4. Click **Create Account**
5. You'll receive a welcome email

### Step 2: Get Seller Role

**Option A: Request from Admin**
1. Contact admin at admin@ecommerce.com
2. Request seller role assignment
3. Provide your business information
4. Wait for admin approval

**Option B: Admin Assigns Role**
1. Admin will assign seller role to your account
2. You'll receive an email notification
3. Logout and login again to activate seller features

### Step 3: Access Seller Dashboard

1. Login with your credentials
2. Navigate to `/seller-rbac`
3. You'll see your Seller Dashboard with four tabs:
   - **Overview**: Statistics and metrics
   - **My Products**: Product management
   - **Orders**: Order management
   - **Business Profile**: Your business information

## Creating Your First Product

### Product Requirements

Before creating a product, ensure you have:
- ✓ High-quality product images (minimum 800x800px)
- ✓ Accurate product description
- ✓ Correct pricing
- ✓ Available stock quantity
- ✓ Appropriate category selection

### Step-by-Step Product Creation

1. **Navigate to My Products Tab**
   - Click on **My Products** in the sidebar
   - Click **Add New Product** button

2. **Fill in Product Details**:

   **Product Name**:
   - Clear and descriptive
   - Include key features
   - Example: "Men's Cotton T-Shirt - Blue - Size L"

   **Description**:
   - Detailed product information
   - Key features and benefits
   - Materials and specifications
   - Care instructions
   - Size guide (if applicable)

   **Price**:
   - Competitive pricing
   - Include currency
   - Consider platform fees
   - Example: 29.99

   **Category**:
   - Select the most appropriate category
   - Available categories: Men, Women, Kids
   - Choose carefully (affects product visibility)

   **Image**:
   - Upload high-quality image
   - Clear product view
   - Good lighting
   - No watermarks
   - Supported formats: JPG, PNG

   **Stock**:
   - Current available quantity
   - Update regularly
   - Set to 0 if out of stock

3. **Review Your Product**:
   - Double-check all information
   - Preview how it will look
   - Ensure image is clear

4. **Submit for Approval**:
   - Click **Add Product**
   - Product status will be "Pending"
   - Wait for admin approval

## Product Approval Process

### How It Works

1. **Submission**: You submit a product
2. **Review**: Admin reviews your product (usually within 24-48 hours)
3. **Decision**: Admin approves or rejects
4. **Notification**: You receive email notification
5. **Live**: If approved, product appears in store immediately

### Approval Criteria

Products are approved based on:
- ✓ **Quality**: High-quality images and descriptions
- ✓ **Accuracy**: Truthful and accurate information
- ✓ **Compliance**: Follows platform policies
- ✓ **Appropriateness**: Suitable for all audiences
- ✓ **Completeness**: All required fields filled

### Common Rejection Reasons

Products may be rejected for:
- ✗ Poor quality images
- ✗ Misleading descriptions
- ✗ Inappropriate content
- ✗ Prohibited items
- ✗ Incorrect pricing
- ✗ Wrong category
- ✗ Copyright violations

### If Your Product is Rejected

1. **Read the Rejection Reason**: Admin will provide specific feedback
2. **Make Corrections**: Fix the issues mentioned
3. **Resubmit**: Edit and resubmit your product
4. **Contact Support**: If you need clarification

**Tips for Faster Approval**:
- Follow all guidelines
- Use high-quality images
- Write detailed descriptions
- Price competitively
- Choose correct category

## Managing Your Products

### Viewing Your Products

1. Go to **My Products** tab
2. You'll see all your products with:
   - Product image
   - Name and price
   - Status (Pending/Approved)
   - Stock level
   - Actions (Edit/Delete)

### Product Statuses

- **Pending**: Awaiting admin approval
- **Approved**: Live in store, customers can purchase
- **Out of Stock**: No stock available

### Editing Products

**To edit a product**:

1. Find the product in your list
2. Click **Edit** button
3. Make your changes
4. Click **Save Changes**
5. If product was approved, changes may require re-approval

**What You Can Edit**:
- Product name
- Description
- Price
- Stock quantity
- Image

**Note**: Major changes may require re-approval

### Deleting Products

**To delete a product**:

1. Find the product in your list
2. Click **Delete** button
3. Confirm deletion
4. Product is permanently removed

**Warning**: Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone!

### Managing Stock

**To update stock levels**:

1. Edit the product
2. Update the **Stock** field
3. Save changes

**Best Practices**:
- Update stock regularly
- Set to 0 when out of stock
- Don't oversell
- Restock popular items quickly

## Managing Orders

### Viewing Your Orders

1. Click on **Orders** tab
2. You'll see orders containing your products with:
   - Order number
   - Customer name
   - Order date
   - Total amount
   - Status
   - Your products in the order

**Important**: You only see orders that contain YOUR products

### Order Statuses

As a seller, you can update orders to these statuses:

1. **Pending**: Order received, needs confirmation
2. **Confirmed**: You've confirmed the order
3. **Processing**: You're preparing the order
4. **Shipped**: You've shipped the order

**Note**: Only delivery staff can mark orders as "Delivered"

### Updating Order Status

**To update an order**:

1. Find the order in your Orders list
2. Click on the order to view details
3. Select new status from dropdown
4. Click **Update Status**
5. Customer is notified automatically

**Status Flow**:
```
Pending → Confirmed → Processing → Shipped → Delivered
```

### Order Responsibilities

As a seller, you must:
- ✓ Confirm orders within 24 hours
- ✓ Process orders within 2-3 business days
- ✓ Ship orders promptly
- ✓ Update status accurately
- ✓ Communicate with customers
- ✓ Handle returns professionally

### Handling Order Issues

**If a customer contacts you**:
1. Respond promptly (within 24 hours)
2. Be professional and courteous
3. Resolve issues fairly
4. Contact support if needed

**Common Issues**:
- Wrong item shipped
- Damaged product
- Late delivery
- Product not as described

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: How long does approval take?**  
A: Usually 24-48 hours. Check your email for updates.

**Q: Can I sell internationally?**  
A: Currently, we only support local sales.

**Q: What are the fees?**  
A: Platform takes a small commission on each sale. Details in your seller agreement.

**Q: How do I get paid?**  
A: Payments are processed monthly to your registered bank account.

### Product Questions

**Q: How many products can I list?**  
A: No limit! List as many as you want.

**Q: Can I edit approved products?**  
A: Yes, but major changes may require re-approval.

**Q: What if my product is rejected?**  
A: Read the rejection reason, make corrections, and resubmit.

**Q: Can I delete a product with active orders?**  
A: No, fulfill all orders first.

### Order Questions

**Q: What if I can't fulfill an order?**  
A: Contact the customer and admin immediately.

**Q: Can I cancel an order?**  
A: Only with customer agreement. Contact support.

**Q: What if a customer wants a refund?**  
A: Follow our refund policy. Contact support for help.

**Q: How do I handle returns?**  
A: Accept returns within 30 days if product is defective.

### Technical Questions

**Q: I can't access the seller dashboard**  
A: Ensure you have seller role. Logout and login again.

**Q: My products aren't showing**  
A: Check if they're approved. Pending products aren't visible to customers.

**Q: I can't upload images**  
A: Check file size (max 5MB) and format (JPG/PNG).

**Q: Order status won't update**  
A: Check your internet connection. Try refreshing the page.
